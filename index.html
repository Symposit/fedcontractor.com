Understanding CMMC Levels

The DoD assigns CMMC levels based on the type of information you handle. Here's what you need to know:

CMMC Level 1

Self-Assessment
  • For contractors handling Federal Contract Information (FCI)
  • 15 basic security requirements
  • Annual self-assessment required
  • Results reported in SPRS
62% of contractors need Level 1

CMMC Level 2

Self or C3PAO Assessment
  • For contractors handling Controlled Unclassified Information (CUI)
  • 110 NIST SP 800-171 controls
  • Self-assessment or third-party assessment
  • Triennial certification required
~37% of contractors need Level 2

CMMC Level 3

DIBCAC Assessment
  • For high-value CUI and critical programs
  • Level 2 + 24 advanced NIST SP 800-172 controls
  • Assessment by DIBCAC only
  • Highest security standards
~1% of contractors need Level 3
